Welcome to InternetSecure.today

We scan the internet on vulnerabilities and security issues and report them.

What we do

Scanning the internet for known vulnerabilities and issues.

Why?

There are a lot of existing vulnerabilities in websites. This endangers millions of users. We believe we make the internet safer by disclosing those issues and allowing website owners to fix the issues.

ANAM

We are using ANAM (Automated Network Analysis en Mass) to scan for known vulnerabilities. ANAM is a beautiful tool written in Go, which we love to work on and uses a custom tcp stack, allowing high scanning speeds.

Enthusiasts

We are security- and technology enthusiasts who loves to build tooling to improve the internet.

How to fix

Most of the time the vulnerablities are caused by small configuration errors which are easy to fix. In our reports we'll include some tips to fix the issues.
