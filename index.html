Welcome to InternetSecure.today

We scan the internet on vulnerabilities and security issues and report them.

What we do

We maintain a huge, real time, fast growing database
with hacker info gathered by state of the art Honeypots.

Why use honeypots?

Honeypots allow you to lure and profile your attackers with great detail and record all evidence to be analyzed.

Immediate response

Honeypots are excellent early-warning systems. Breaches and suspicious interest in your networks can be detected immediately.

How to use

Install your own high-interaction honeypots and analyze your data via the Honeytrap portal or via the reporting services.

Honeycast portal

Manage all your honeypot installs and investigate all your honeycast sessions, all in one place.

Customizable

All kinds of honeypot environments are available or can be created. For example a vulnerable Wordpress environment.

Container Technology

Our high-interaction honeypots are based on a container technology, each attacker will get it`s own personal environment.

Extensive logs

All traffic and files are being saved and analyzed. Memory dump is created, for the forensic analysis.
